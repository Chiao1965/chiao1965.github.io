Ya-Chiao Yang

I am currently studying for an MBA degree in information management at National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University. The main research areas of the laboratory are network communication and information security. The topic I'm interested at are resource allocation and attack-defense methods in cloud/fog environments.
The main research areas were DL and ML during my undergraduate studies. I was a member of the Computational Biology & Intelligence System Lab supervised by Prof. Tzu-Hsien Yang. At that time, I worked as a research assistant for my professor and publish three journal papers, including NLP, CNN and database.
